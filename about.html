About Me

I am a multilingual and multidisciplinary Web Engineer who is about to obtain Germany's official IHK-Degree on Computer Science. Additionally I hold a Bachelor of Science in International Relations.

I have studied and worked in 4 languages including English and German. After working in 6 countries, living in 10 cities, working in fields of Academy, Linguistics, Tourism, Cinema and Performing Arts both as an employee and an independent freelancer, I settled in Berlin in 2017.

To pursue a career in Programming I have started a shortened 2-year German vocational retraining school on Computer Science with subject area of Software Development. Since July 2019 I have been part-time working as a Software Developer Intern at a digital communication company.

Vue.js, JavaScript and Node.js are my actual weapons of choice, but I am also interested in many others.

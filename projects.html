Libsta

The objective of the project is to cover the need in Berlin for an alternative library since German libraries do not have sufficient number of books in other languages. With this web app, people will be able to connect their own libraries to one another and not only exchange books in a large community but also get in touch with people, whom they share a common book taste.

Build With: JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, Pug, Vue.js, Bootstrap, Mongoose, Axios
